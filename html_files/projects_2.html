HikeMates

HikeMates is a WebApp to connect people who want to hike together and build a community.

Projects Link

Project Overview

HikeMates is a dynamic social platform for hiking enthusiasts designed to foster safe and enjoyable hiking experiences. Users can join or create hiking groups, track upcoming hikes on a calendar, and engage with other users through features like friend requests, group chats, and event notifications. The platform allows users to share their hike achievements, photos, and reviews.

Key Features:

  • Group Creation and Management: Users can create or join public and private hiking groups, complete with chat capabilities, itinerary, and member management.
  • Share Photos: Users can share their hike photos on their profile in a gallery type manner.
  • Review System: A user can provide reviews on the location, the trial or the hike that they went on. Like/dislike or comment features.
  • Two Types of Hike:
    • Public: If it is a public group then the user can immediately join the group.
    • Private: If it is a private hike group then the user needs to send a request to join.
  • Friend Requests and Community Building: Users can connect with fellow hikers, send friend requests, and share hike details, fostering community spirit.
